How It Works

  • We begin by discussing your specific business needs. Whether you're focused on social media growth, lead generation, or both, we'll create a tailored strategy for your brand.
  • Once the strategy is set, we begin the ad campaign. You'll provide content for the ads, ensuring the messaging is aligned with your brand voice. Afterward, we'll take care of the rest by running the campaigns and optimizing for the best performance.
  • There are two main fees involved: the service fee for our expertise in managing and optimizing your campaigns, and the ad budget that you allocate for the ads themselves.
  • Your ad budget will go directly to the platform's advertising services (such as Facebook Ads, Google Ads, etc.) and is separate from our service fees.
